The weather in my city changes very quickly during the spring. One day the sun is shining and people walk in the park, but the next day heavy rain and strong wind return again. Many people enjoy this season because everything becomes green and fresh. Trees grow new leaves, flowers begin to open, and the days become longer. Children spend more time outside after school and ride their bicycles with friends.

In the morning I usually drink tea and look through the window before I go to work. If the sky is clear, I feel more energetic and positive. When the clouds are dark, I prefer to stay at home in the evening and read a book. Weather has a strong influence on daily life, mood, and activity. Even simple plans often depend on temperature and rain.

Many families like to travel in spring because the air is pleasant and the roads are less crowded than in summer. Some people visit the countryside, while others choose famous cities and museums. No matter where they go, they usually enjoy the fresh air and the new colors of nature.
