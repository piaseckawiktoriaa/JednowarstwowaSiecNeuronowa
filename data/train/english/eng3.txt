A healthy lifestyle is based on several simple habits. First, people should eat regular meals and include vegetables, fruit, and enough water in their daily diet. Second, physical activity is very important. It does not always mean hard training at the gym. A long walk, a short run, or even cycling to work can improve health and reduce stress.

Sleep also plays a major role in maintaining good condition. When people sleep too little, they often feel tired, unfocused, and irritated. Good rest helps the brain and the body recover after a busy day. It is also useful to reduce time spent on screens before going to bed.

Mental health is just as important as physical health. Relaxation, hobbies, and contact with other people have a positive effect on well being. Spending time with family or close friends can make difficult days easier. Small healthy choices repeated every day often produce better results than sudden and unrealistic plans.
