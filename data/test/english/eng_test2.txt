Traveling by train is comfortable and practical for many people. Passengers can read, work, or simply look through the window during the journey. Unlike driving a car, traveling by train allows them to relax and avoid traffic. It is often a good solution for students, workers, and tourists.

Railway stations are usually located near city centers, which makes travel easier. People can continue their trip by bus, tram, or on foot. Because of that, trains remain an important form of transport in many countries.
